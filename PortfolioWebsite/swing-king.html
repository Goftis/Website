Swing King

Project Overview

A GMTK 2025 Game Jam entry, under the theme "Loop".
It's a game about a monkey escaping the forest fires while swinging in loops towards safety.

My Role

I managed the project's scope and ambitions, whether in deadlines or in implemented mechanics and systems.
Yoav and I both built the code for the game, I worked specifically on the player's movement and attached systems.
Designed the level and the difficulty curve while trying to balance it with the player's enjoyment.
